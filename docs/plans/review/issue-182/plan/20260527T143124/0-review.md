**Verdict**

Not ready as written. The process-chunk framing is coherent, and the `restore` and two-layer reclaim/reassign reconciliations are basically right, but §4 currently overclaims completeness and would miss real operator surfaces. The biggest problems are the Adminer reconciliation and an incomplete surface inventory.

**Blocking**

- The plan treats Adminer as a missing surface that should automatically yield a finding, but the repo already documents Adminer as a real operator workflow: a bring-your-own sibling container on the EDEN network, not a built-in Compose service. As written, §1.2/§4.2 would skip auditing that documented workflow and instead force a bogus “no Adminer” issue. See the plan’s current stance in [issue-182-coverage-debt-audit.md](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-182-coverage-debt/docs/plans/issue-182-coverage-debt-audit.md:23) and the existing operator docs in [observability.md](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-182-coverage-debt/docs/observability.md:206) and [user-guide.md](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-182-coverage-debt/docs/user-guide.md:390). `restore` being unblocked and reclaim/reassign existing at both UI and wire layers are accurate; the Adminer conclusion is the part that is not.
