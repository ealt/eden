## Plan
- Path: docs/plans/eden-phase-12a-2-orchestrator-as-role.md
- Summary: Convert the orchestrator from singleton process to a role: zero, one, or many instances may run concurrently as registered workers in the `orchestrators` group. Decisions are gated per-experiment by a `dispatch_mode` field (4 normative keys: `ideation_creation` / `execution_dispatch` / `evaluation_dispatch` / `integration`, each `auto` or `manual`). Operators get first-class wire ops to reassign tasks and flip dispatch_mode on a live experiment.
