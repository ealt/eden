Most of my round-0 concerns are resolved. The revised plan now correctly treats wave 2 as an app-wide identity refactor, makes the CLI recovery/admin-token dependency explicit, separates stale-token recovery from row-gone-after-rebootstrap, and acknowledges that per-session bearer routing needs a store-factory abstraction rather than a naïve `StoreClient` constructor.

Two remaining implementation-contract gaps still look material:

1. The `admin_store is None` posture is now underspecified.
   - The revised plan makes both signup and login depend on `app.state.admin_store` [issue-140 plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/issue-140-operator-identity-worker.md:112), [issue-140 plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/issue-140-operator-identity-worker.md:113), and it also moves the `/admin/*` membership check onto `admin_store` [issue-140 plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/issue-140-operator-identity-worker.md:127).
   - But the current web-ui has a documented and tested “admin-disabled” posture where `admin_store is None`, admin writes are disabled, and read paths still work [README.md](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/reference/services/web-ui/README.md:96), [README.md](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/reference/services/web-ui/README.md:98), [conftest.py](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/reference/services/web-ui/tests/conftest.py:115).
   - The updated plan does not say what `/signup`, `/login`, or `/admin/*` should do when `admin_store` is absent after #140. That is a real contract question now, because #140 changes “no admin token” from “admin writes disabled” into “auth establishment may be impossible” and “admin gate may not be evaluable.”
   - The plan should state one of these explicitly:
     - the web-ui now hard-requires `--admin-token` / `EDEN_ADMIN_TOKEN` at startup after #140, or
     - `/signup` and `/login` render a clear admin-disabled error posture when `admin_store is None`, and `/admin/*` has a defined read-disabled/error behavior rather than inheriting the old read-only posture.

2. The setup-experiment idempotency story still needs a canonical persisted source for the seeded operator’s opaque id.
   - The plan correctly notes that after #128, `register_worker(name=…)` mints an opaque id and names may collide, so rerunning setup cannot do a blind name-based re-register [issue-140 plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/issue-140-operator-identity-worker.md:165).
   - But #128 is explicit that names may collide and are not uniquely resolvable [identity plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/identity-id-name-disambiguation.md:51), [identity plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/identity-id-name-disambiguation.md:52). The new `?name=` query is 0..N, not a unique lookup [identity plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/identity-id-name-disambiguation.md:400), [identity plan](/Users/ericalt/Documents/eden-worktrees/plan-issue-140-operator-identity-worker/docs/plans/identity-id-name-disambiguation.md:401).
   - So “capture-or-lookup” is still too loose as a contract. The plan should say where the captured seeded operator `worker_id` is durably persisted and reread on rerun. A new env var or metadata record is the obvious answer; name lookup alone is not sufficient.

Everything else I flagged in round 0 looks adequately addressed in the revised draft. If you tighten those two points, I’d consider this ready as an implementation contract.
